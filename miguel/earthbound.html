Earthbound

A friend of mine, the artist Adie, prepared to release his first full album and I was brought on the collaborate on the visual palette and story for the album. “Earthbound” is a concept album that takes listeners on a virtual trip to outer space.

I wanted to connect Adie’s experience in New York to these themes of belonging and isolation. We took this character into the 3 music videos I directed for the album. I brought on DP’s for the first two videos, and shot “Elephant” myself.
